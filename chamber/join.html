Membership Levels

NP Membership

Non-profit organizations are welcome to join the Fred Chamber of Commerce at no cost. Benefits include:

  • Access to chamber events and networking opportunities
  • Listing in the chamber's online directory
  • Participation in chamber committees and working groups
  • Access to educational resources and workshops

Bronze Membership

For $50 a year, Bronze Members receive:

  • All benefits of NP Membership
  • Discounted rates for chamber events and programs
  • Opportunity to host or sponsor chamber events
  • Access to targeted marketing and advertising opportunities
  • Business referrals and leads from the chamber
  • Access to chamber's business advocacy and lobbying efforts

Silver Membership

For $100 a year, Silver Members receive:

  • All benefits of Bronze Membership
  • Enhanced business listing in the chamber's online directory
  • Exclusive access to chamber's business resources and reports
  • Invitations to VIP networking events and receptions
  • Opportunity to present at chamber seminars and workshops
  • Representation in the chamber's marketing and promotional materials
  • Recognition as a Silver Member in chamber publications

Gold Membership

For $200 a year, Gold Members receive:

  • All benefits of Silver Membership
  • Priority listing and featured promotion in the chamber's online directory
  • Exclusive sponsorship opportunities for signature chamber events
  • Leadership opportunities within the chamber's board of directors
  • Invitations to private receptions with community leaders and dignitaries
  • Access to specialized business training and executive education programs
  • Recognition as a Gold Member in chamber publications and press releases
  • Opportunity to showcase products or services at chamber trade shows
